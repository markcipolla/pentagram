# Google Play Store Listing

## App Name
Pentagram - AirPlay Receiver

## Short Description (80 characters max)
Mirror your iPhone, iPad, or Mac screen to any Android device via AirPlay.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Transform your Android device into an AirPlay receiver! Pentagram lets you wirelessly mirror your iPhone, iPad, or Mac screen directly to your Android phone or tablet.

**Key Features:**

• Screen Mirroring - Mirror your Apple device's screen in real-time
• No Apple TV Required - Use any Android device as an AirPlay display
• Local Network Only - All streaming happens on your WiFi, nothing goes to the cloud
• Open Source - Fully transparent, GPL v3 licensed
• No Ads - Clean, distraction-free experience
• No Account Required - Just install and start mirroring
• Privacy Focused - No data collection, no analytics, no tracking

**How It Works:**

1. Install Pentagram on your Android device
2. Connect both devices to the same WiFi network
3. On your Apple device, open Control Center
4. Tap "Screen Mirroring" and select your Android device
5. Start mirroring!

**Perfect For:**

• Presentations - Show your iPhone or iPad on a larger Android screen
• Gaming - Mirror mobile games to a bigger display
• Photo Sharing - Display photos and videos from your iPhone
• Development - Test iOS apps on an Android display
• Education - Share educational content across platforms

**Technical Details:**

• Supports iOS 12+ and macOS 10.15+
• Requires Android 7.0 (Nougat) or later
• Works on phones, tablets, and Android TV
• Hardware-accelerated H.264 video decoding
• Low latency streaming

**Open Source:**

Pentagram is free and open-source software. View the source code, report issues, or contribute at our GitHub repository.
